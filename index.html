FAQs

'Mingbai Mandarin' is a website developed for the thesis that offers a revolutionary approach to language learning. Mingbai （明白）, in Chinese, simply means to understand, and Mandarin, known as Putonghua（普通话） by native speakers, means 'common language'. Mandarin is the dialect of Chinese and it is the most spoken around the world. Therefore, Mingbai Mandarin means "Understanding Mandarin.

Mingbai Mandarin is a Chinese/Mandarin learning website which uses Games to make learning more interesting and fun.

To learn Chinese/Mandarin using Mingbai Mandarin, just click on PLAY button or link the top to visit the page where different games are listed for users to play and learn Chinese/Mandarin while playing.
